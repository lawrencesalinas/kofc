Welcome to Our Council

St. Kevin Council 18180 is a brotherhood of Catholic men dedicated to serving our parish, community, and families. Founded in 2023, we bring together men of faith who want to make a real difference through charitable works, prayer, and fellowship.

Whether you're looking to grow in your faith, serve others, or build lasting friendships with like-minded Catholic men, you'll find a home here.

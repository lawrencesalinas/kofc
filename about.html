About Our Council

Brotherhood founded on faith, service, and charity

Who We Are

The Knights of Columbus are Catholic men of faith and men of charitable action. Membership in the Knights of Columbus provides an association with brotherly companions. It offers the opportunity for fellowship with those of the same belief who recognize the same duty to God, to family and to neighbor, and who stand with those who share those beliefs.

Programs are organized to appeal to the individual interests of the members and to enable them to render service to their church, their country, and their fellow men. Regularly-scheduled times of prayer and devotion are also part of the Knights' charism, helping brothers to grow deeper in their relationship with Jesus.

St. Kevin Council 18180 was founded in 2023 with the goal of providing additional support and volunteerism to St. Kevin Parish and the Auburndale Community. We offer men a place to lead their families and communities in faith and service and put their Faith into Action.
